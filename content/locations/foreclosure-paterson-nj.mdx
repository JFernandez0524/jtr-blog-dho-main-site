## Your Options in Paterson

**Protect your equity.** Paterson's diverse housing stock — from historic mill-town homes to multi-family properties — has real value in the right hands. If you have equity, selling before the process runs its course protects far more of it than a completed sheriff's sale.

**Sell to a vetted cash buyer.** Paterson has significant multi-family and older-home inventory, sometimes with tenant or repair complications. I maintain a list of vetted investors who pay fair market value — not lowball numbers — and can move faster than a traditional listing.

**Short sale.** If you owe more than the home is worth, a short sale with lender approval typically does far less damage to your credit than a completed foreclosure.

**Loan modification.** If keeping the home is the goal, it's worth exploring a restructured payment plan with your lender before assuming a sale is your only option.

## Watch Out for "Foreclosure Rescue" Offers

Once a foreclosure becomes public record, Paterson homeowners often get targeted with letters and calls promising to "save your home." Be cautious of anyone asking for money upfront or wanting you to sign your deed over — those are scam patterns, not real help.
