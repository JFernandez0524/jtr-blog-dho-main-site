## Foreclosure in Camden County

Cherry Hill is part of Camden County, so foreclosure cases here move through the **Camden County Superior Court, Chancery Division**, and a sale — if a case reaches that point — would be conducted through the **Camden County Sheriff's Office**.

Every homeowner's situation moves at its own pace based on where things stand with your lender and the court. Instead of quoting generic timeframes that may not apply to you, the most useful thing I can offer is an honest read on your specific situation.

## Your Options in Cherry Hill

**Protect your equity.** Cherry Hill's high-demand market means many homeowners here have real equity built up. If that's you, selling before the process runs its course protects far more of that equity than a completed sheriff's sale would.

**Sell to a vetted cash buyer.** When speed matters more than maximizing price, I maintain a list of vetted investors who pay fair market value — not lowball numbers — and can move faster than a traditional listing.

**Short sale.** If you owe more than the home is worth, a short sale with lender approval typically does far less damage to your credit than a completed foreclosure.

**Loan modification.** If keeping the home is the priority, it's worth exploring a restructured payment plan with your lender before assuming a sale is the only option.

## Watch Out for "Foreclosure Rescue" Offers

Once a foreclosure becomes public record, Cherry Hill homeowners often get targeted with letters and calls promising to "save your home." Be cautious of anyone asking for money upfront or wanting you to sign your deed over — those are scam patterns, not real help.
