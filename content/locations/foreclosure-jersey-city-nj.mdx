## Four Real Paths for Jersey City Owners

**Full market sale.** Jersey City's buyer pool is deep and fast-moving. If time allows, this nets the most — and I list here regularly.

**Vetted cash buyer.** When a sale date looms, my investor list pays fair market value on Jersey City property — walkups, tenant-occupied two-families, estate-condition rowhomes included — without the lowball tactics. Speed is the point.

**Short sale.** Less common here because values have run so far, but if you refinanced at the top and owe more than today's value, lender approval for a short sale protects your credit far better than a completed foreclosure.

**Modification and stay.** Especially where rental income from a second unit helps carry the mortgage, restructuring sometimes works. I'll tell you straight whether your numbers support it.
