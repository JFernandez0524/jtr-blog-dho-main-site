## Probate in Toms River

Inherited properties in Toms River go through probate at the **Ocean County Surrogate's Court**, located at 118 Washington Street in Toms River. The process typically takes 6–12 months, during which the estate is responsible for:

- Property taxes (Ocean County rates are moderate but add up)
- Homeowner's insurance
- Maintenance and utilities
- Any outstanding mortgage balance

Acting early — even before probate closes — can help you understand your timeline and avoid unnecessary carrying costs.

## Your Options in Toms River

### Sell Traditionally
Toms River's proximity to the Jersey Shore and its large retiree population create consistent demand. A well-priced, properly marketed home can attract strong offers, especially in neighborhoods like Silver Ridge, Holiday City, and Silverton.

### Sell As-Is
Many inherited homes in Toms River are older properties that need updating. Selling as-is to an investor or cash buyer removes the burden of repairs and can close in weeks rather than months.

### Keep the Property
Toms River's rental market is solid, particularly for single-family homes near Barnegat Bay. If the estate can carry the costs, holding the property as a rental is worth evaluating.
