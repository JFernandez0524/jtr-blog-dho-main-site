## Foreclosure in Monmouth County

Freehold Borough is the county seat of Monmouth County, so foreclosure cases involving Freehold-area properties move through the **Monmouth County Superior Court, Chancery Division**, and — if a case reaches that point — a sale would be conducted through the **Monmouth County Sheriff's Office**.

Every case is different, and the timeline depends on where things stand for you specifically — how far behind you are, whether anything's been filed yet, whether you've been in contact with your lender. Rather than guess at generic timeframes here, the most useful thing I can do is look at your specific situation with you and tell you honestly where you stand and what's still possible.

## Your Options in Freehold

**Protect your equity.** Freehold's housing stock — from the historic homes near downtown to the newer developments further out — has held value well. If you have equity in your home, selling before the process runs its course is almost always the way to protect the most of it; a completed sheriff's sale is the outcome that costs homeowners the most.

**Sell to a vetted cash buyer.** When speed matters more than maximizing price, I maintain a list of vetted investors who pay fair market value — not lowball "we buy houses" numbers — which can move faster than a traditional listing.

**Short sale.** If you owe more than the home is worth, a short sale with lender approval typically does far less damage to your credit than a completed foreclosure.

**Loan modification.** Sometimes the right answer is keeping the home — restructuring payments with your lender is worth exploring before assuming a sale is your only option.

## Watch Out for "Foreclosure Rescue" Offers

Once a foreclosure becomes public record, Freehold homeowners often start getting letters and calls promising to "save your home." Be cautious of anyone who asks for money upfront or wants you to sign your deed over — those are the classic patterns of a scam, not a solution. A licensed, verifiable agent never asks for payment before helping you.
