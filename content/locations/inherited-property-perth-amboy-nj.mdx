## What the Perth Amboy Market Looks Like Right Now

Homes here are selling at **100.94% of list price** on average — meaning the typical Perth Amboy home now sells at or above asking.

The **median sold price reached $471,000** in June 2026, up 5.8% from the prior month, while the median list price sits at $460,000 — another sign that buyers are bidding past asking to win homes. Perth Amboy sellers moved roughly **$5.86 million in total sales volume** in a single month, at a median of **$316 per square foot**. Longer-term, the median estimated single-family home value in Perth Amboy is **$531,250, up 1.3% over the past 12 months** — steady appreciation on top of an already competitive market.

For estate and inherited-property sellers, one more number matters: Perth Amboy currently has **30 pre-foreclosures and only a handful of distressed sales** in the pipeline. Investors know this market is supply-starved — which is exactly why the "we buy houses" offers arriving in your mailbox are priced well below what competing retail buyers will actually pay.

A properly priced and marketed inherited home in Perth Amboy today attracts competing buyers — even in original condition. The mistake is assuming a dated house has to go cheap to a wholesaler. *(Market data: Realtors Property Resource, June 2026, single-family, Perth Amboy 08861.)*
