## Probate in Freehold

Inherited properties in Freehold go through probate at the **Monmouth County Surrogate's Court**. The process can take several months, and during that time the property continues to generate costs:

- Property taxes
- Insurance
- Maintenance
- Utilities
- Any outstanding mortgage

Understanding what you're responsible for — and for how long — is the first step.

## Your Options in Freehold

### Sell Traditionally
Freehold's market is active, and a properly prepared home can sell well. This path takes longer but can maximize your return if the property is in good condition.

### Sell As-Is
If the home needs repairs or you need to close quickly, selling as-is removes the burden of renovations. I work with buyers who purchase homes in any condition.

### Keep the Property
Some heirs choose to keep the home as a rental or primary residence. Freehold's central Monmouth County location makes it a solid long-term hold.
