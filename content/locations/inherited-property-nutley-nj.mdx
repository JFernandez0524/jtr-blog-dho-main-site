Nutley estates go through the **Essex County Surrogate's Court** in Newark. ## What Makes Nutley Inheritances Distinctive

Nutley is a small, tight-knit township where homes often stay in one family for generations — which means inherited homes here are frequently original-condition: solid bones, dated kitchens, forty years of memories in the closets. That's not a problem; it's a known quantity. Buyers compete hard for Nutley — commuters chasing the town's parks, schools, and proximity to Manhattan — and they do so for updated homes *and* project homes alike. The question is which sale path nets your family more.
