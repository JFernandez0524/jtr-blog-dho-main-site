**Cherry Hill, New Jersey** is one of South Jersey's most sought-after communities — and inheriting property here means you're dealing with a high-demand market where the right strategy can make a significant difference in your outcome.

## Probate in Cherry Hill

Cherry Hill is in Camden County, so inherited properties go through probate at the **Camden County Surrogate's Court**, located at 101 South Fifth Street in Camden. The process typically takes 6–12 months, and the estate is responsible for all carrying costs during that time.

Cherry Hill's property taxes are substantial — understanding your monthly obligations from the moment you inherit is essential to avoiding financial strain during the probate period.

## Your Options in Cherry Hill

### Sell Traditionally
Cherry Hill's strong school system, central South Jersey location, and established neighborhoods like Barclay Farm, Erlton, and Woodcrest attract consistent buyer demand. A properly prepared home in Cherry Hill can sell quickly and at a strong price.

### Sell As-Is
Many Cherry Hill estates involve homes built in the 1960s–1980s that need updating. Selling as-is to an investor or cash buyer is a practical option when the estate can't fund renovations or when heirs need a fast resolution.

### Keep the Property
Cherry Hill's rental market is solid, driven by proximity to Philadelphia and major employers. If the estate can manage the property, holding it as a rental is worth evaluating.
