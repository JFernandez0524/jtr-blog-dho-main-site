## Your Options in Woodbridge

**Protect your equity.** Woodbridge's strategic location at the intersection of the Garden State Parkway and the Turnpike has kept demand consistent. If you have equity, selling before the process runs its course protects far more of it than a completed sheriff's sale.

**Sell to a vetted cash buyer.** When speed matters more than maximizing price, I maintain a list of vetted investors who pay fair market value — not lowball numbers — and can move faster than a traditional listing.

## Watch Out for "Foreclosure Rescue" Offers

Once a foreclosure becomes public record, Woodbridge homeowners often get targeted with letters and calls promising to "save your home." Be cautious of anyone asking for money upfront or wanting you to sign your deed over — those are scam patterns, not real help.
